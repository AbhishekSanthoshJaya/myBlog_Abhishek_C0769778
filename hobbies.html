HOBBIES

GAMING

From a young age, I loved playing games. When I was 10 years old, I started playing YugiOh (a trading card game) competitively. By the time I was 15 years old, I had made over $800 dollars in prize money and cards, which seemed like a lot at the time. I gave it up a few years later to focus on my studies. In 2012 I got into call of duty 4 and started playing for an international team. I made a lot of friends all over the world. I took over co-leadership and website maintenance for them a few years later. You can check out our website here!
